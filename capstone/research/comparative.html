Comparative Analysis

1. Climate Future – The Tardigrade

Interface Design: The site uses colorful and strong visual storytelling.The rainbow style color matches the trend of climate change. Since the temprature rises, the color turns to orange-ish or red-ish.

Interaction Design: Interactive infographics and subtle transitions improve engagement. Navigation is intuitive and encourages deep exploration. There is a straightforward introduction to guide users to start

User Experience: The project clearly explains climate data in a way that is accessible to all audiences and varies based on time

2. SKYGLOW Project

Interface Design: The dark theme and stunning photography highlight the beauty of unpolluted night skies versus the effects of city lights.

Interaction Design: Responsive layout and multimedia integration create a rich browsing experience across all devices.

User Experience: The project merges art and science to emotionally engage users and drive awareness about light pollution’s ecological effects. But it is more informative than interactive
